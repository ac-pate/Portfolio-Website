## Overview

Custom drone designed for forest fire prevention and safety missions, competing in the NARC UAV competition.

## Features

### Navigation & Control

- GPS-based mission planning & pathfinding
- Altitude hold using optical flow sensors
- FPV head-tracking gimbal for situational awareness
- PID tuning and sensor data filtering for flight stability

### Computer Vision

- Fire detection using MediaPipe and thermal imaging
- Obstacle avoidance system
- Real-time video processing and streaming

### Gesture Control (Personal Challenge)

- TinyML implementation on microcontroller
- Gesture recognition for mode switching
- Toggle between FPV head-tracking and gesture-controlled modes

## Competition

Competing in NARC UAV competition focusing on autonomous forest fire prevention missions.
